# Pre-Dig Checklist

**Classification:** `public-safe` — generic procedure, no site-identifying information.
**Use:** before any excavation — mast base, guy anchors, ground rods/electrode, or conduit trenching.

> 🔒 **STOP conditions.** Do **not** break ground until every 🔒 item is satisfied. Underground
> utility strikes and unstable excavations are life-safety hazards. When in doubt, do not dig.

---

## 1. Utility locate (do this FIRST)

- [ ] 🔒 Called **811** (US "Call Before You Dig") — or the local equivalent — and opened a locate
      ticket **the required lead time in advance** (varies by jurisdiction; confirm locally).
- [ ] 🔒 All public utilities marked (electric, gas, water, sewer, telecom/fiber, cable).
- [ ] 🔒 **Private** underground lines located too — 811 does **not** cover private runs (well,
      septic, irrigation, buried propane, sub-panels, landscape lighting, prior owner installs).
      Arrange a private locate if any are possible.
- [ ] 🔒 Locate marks are current/unexpired and photographed for the record (private repo; strip
      EXIF/landmarks before any public use).
- [ ] 🔒 Hand-dig / vacuum-excavate ("potholing") within the tolerance zone of any marked line.

## 2. Permits, code & authority

- [ ] 🔒 Confirmed whether a **permit** is required (foundation/structural, electrical/grounding,
      right-of-way/setback). Obtained if required.
- [ ] 🔒 Confirmed whether **structural review** (mast foundation, guy anchors) is required by the
      local authority — engage a licensed professional if so.
- [ ] Setback / easement / HOA constraints checked against the **private** site plan.

## 3. Field verification (not off GIS/conceptual data) 🔎

- [ ] 🔎 Mast base, guy-anchor, and trench positions **physically staked and measured on site** —
      not scaled from public GIS, aerial imagery, or conceptual drawings.
- [ ] 🔎 Anchor geometry (radius, spacing, bearing) verified against the mast/guy design on the
      actual ground, accounting for slope and obstructions.
- [ ] 🔎 Conduit route walked; clearances to structures, drainage, and located utilities confirmed.

## 4. Ground conditions & safety

- [ ] Excavation depth and any shoring/spoil-pile plan reviewed (deep holes can collapse).
- [ ] Soil/rock/water-table conditions considered for foundation and anchor holding.
- [ ] Weather window checked; no digging in unsafe conditions.
- [ ] Overhead hazards checked — 🔒 **overhead power lines** vs. mast height and raising path.
- [ ] PPE, tools, and spotter/second-person present for anchor and mast-adjacent work.

## 5. Records

- [ ] Locate ticket number, permit numbers, and pro sign-offs recorded (private repo).
- [ ] Pre-dig photos captured for as-builts (private; sanitize before any public use).
